How to use SignalCatch
  1. Open Scanner and scan the top Bybit USDT perpetuals. The default is 100 symbols and you can change it from 10 to 200.
  2. Expand Current setups for a symbol. Review every timeframe before making a decision.
  3. Entry zone, SL, and TP are current-price analysis values. Daily and swing setups also show split entries and split TP guidance.
  4. Use Backtest as an optional secondary check. Historical win rate is not a promise about the next trade.
  5. SignalCatch is an analysis dashboard. It does not place orders and is not financial advice.
